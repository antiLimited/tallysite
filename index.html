What is Tally Hall?

Tally Hall is a "fabloo" rock band formed in Ann Arbor, Michigan by 5 college friends who wanted to start a band. After releasing many EPs and Demos, Tally Hall released their first album "Marvin's Marvelous Mechanical Museum" on November 22, 2005, which they rereleased on April Fools Day of 2008 with two new bonus tracks and most of the songs reworked. After many more years of production, they eventually released their second album known as "Good & Evil" on June 21, 2011, and shortly after touring they went on an indefinite hiatus which they are still yet to emerge from. All 5 of the members went on to pursue their own goals (some music and some not) during the band's hiatus, leading to many side projects, and that is where we stand today.
